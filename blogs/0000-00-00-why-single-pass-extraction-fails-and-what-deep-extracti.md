Single-pass document extraction fails because it lacks verification mechanisms to catch errors like dropped rows or consolidated entries. Deep extraction solves this through an iterative, agent-driven approach that verifies output against source documents until quality thresholds are met, achieving 99-100% accuracy instead of 10-20% on complex, high-stakes documents.
