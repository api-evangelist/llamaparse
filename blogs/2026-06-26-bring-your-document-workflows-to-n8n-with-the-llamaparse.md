LlamaIndex released versions 5 and 6 of the LlamaParse Platform node for n8n, an officially verified community node enabling document intelligence in no-code workflows. The node surfaces five LlamaCloud resources—Parse, Classify, Extract, Split, and Retrieve—allowing users to build document processing pipelines like classification-to-extraction workflows or retrieval-augmented AI agents.
