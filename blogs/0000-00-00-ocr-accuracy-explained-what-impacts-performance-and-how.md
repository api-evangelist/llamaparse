OCR accuracy is not a single metric; it depends on measuring character error rate, word error rate, or field-level accuracy. Real-world performance on business documents often falls short of vendor benchmarks because success depends on the entire pipeline: input quality, preprocessing, the OCR engine, and post-processing validation with language models.
